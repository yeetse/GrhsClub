Welcome to the GRHS Automotive Enthusiast Club!

Who Are We?

We are a club fuelled by our passion for cars. Through car meets and engaging discussions and debates, we embrace the thrill of the open road, sharing knowledge, and igniting the engine of camaraderie.

What Do We Do?

We indulge in thrilling car meets and engage in captivating discussions and debates ranging from the latest automotive news and advancements to what types of engines and cars are best. We also discuss upcoming race weekends in motorsports from Formula One to IndyCar to IMSA, discussing what strategy teams and drivers should use to approach the race from what tires should be used to how many pit stops should be taken.

Where And When Do We Meet?

We meet once a month on Thursday from 3:50 to 4:20 in Room 2308 (Mr. Ryburn's Room)

Our Next Discussion Topic:

Historically, Toyota and Porsche have dominated the World Endurance Championship (WEC) LMP1 category including the famed 24 hours of Le Mans. This was because the LMP1 category became so expensive that manufacturers like Audi, Nissan, Lotus, and eventually Porsche left WEC altogether. However, starting in 2021, the LMP1 category was replaced by the Le Mans Hypercar (LMH) category, which was far less expensive than the LMP1 category, allowing for more manufacturers. This has ushered in a new "Golden Era" for endurance racing as nearly 10 manufacturers are competing in the LMH category with notables like Acura, BMW, Cadillac, Lamborghini, and of course Toyota and Porsche. Do you believe that this new era of endurance racing will stop the dominance enjoyed by Toyota and Porsche?
